I am excited to announce that my proposal for the NSF-Simons Center for Multiscale Cell Fate Interdisciplinary Opporunity Award [(CMCF-IOA)](https://cellfate.uci.edu/) proposal was funded! Together with my collaborators Dr. Jun Allard and Sohyeon Park in the UC Irvine Department of Mathematics, we aim to uncover gene-regulatory alterations inAlzheimer's Disease by constructing gene regulatory networks using multi-omic single-cell data, and perform in-silico perturbation analyses to infer how healthy cell states transition to disease.
