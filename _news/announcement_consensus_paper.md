My first first-author paper was published in Human Molecular Genetics! In this study we applied integrative genomics approaches to multiple cohorts of AD patients to identify gold-standard gene co-expression networks, followed by deep characterization using orthogonal datasets such as DNA methylation, GWAS, and QTLs. Read the manuscript [here](https://doi.org/10.1093/hmg/ddaa182).
