A network analytic study of the proteome in neurodegeneration from my advisor Dr. Vivek Swarup and his colleagues in the [Geschwind lab at UCLA](https://geschwindlab.dgsom.ucla.edu/pages/)
was just published in [Cell Reports](https://www.biorxiv.org/content/10.1101/2020.05.11.088591v1). I developed a R-shiny application to accompany this study to easily explore this complex dataset.
Check out the [shiny app here](http://swaruplab.bio.uci.edu:3838/ADpNet/) on the Swarup lab website.
