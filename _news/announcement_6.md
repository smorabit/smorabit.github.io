Recently my peers and I have founded [genPALS](https://uci-genpals.github.io/), where UC Irvine genomics researchers
come together to discuss current methods and best practices for bioinformatic data analysis. I had the privilege
of giving the first talk of the seminar series, in which we discussed scVelo. Check out the [accompanying
tutorial](https://uci-genpals.github.io/pseudotime/2021/02/09/scvelo-tutorial.html) going over how to use scVelo to analye transcriptional dynamics (RNA velocity) using scRNA-seq datasets.
